God of War Ragnarok-
Everything We Know So Far

In terms of story, God of War Ragnarok appears to take place directly after the prequel's harrowing climax. Though we'll likely revisit familiar areas, it's expected that you'll be able to explore all Nine Realms of Norse Mythology. To that end, we suggest going back to God of War and playing it on the PS5, as the enhancements it offers can take the seminal adventure and make it feel even better.
